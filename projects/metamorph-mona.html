Metamorph M.O.N.A.

A generative composition built from the letters of my name, exploring material intelligence, motion, and atmosphere through Grasshopper-based forms, controlled color palettes, and immersive light reflections.

Concept & Letter-Based Morphology

The models in this work were created using letters of the English alphabet in Grasshopper. Four letters were used to form this model. By applying a limited palette of cool grays complemented by selective blue and warm tones, the piece gains both depth and atmosphere. This restrained use of color creates a professional, cohesive, and immersive environment.

The oblique forms convey a sense of dynamism and motion, emphasizing fluidity and transformation throughout the composition. The model consists of the letters M, O, N, and A representing my name, Mona.

My background is not in architecture. However, my curiosity and passion for this field led me to explore it through design. This concept reflects my evolving interest, expressing the strong sense of movement, creativity, and direction that guide me in this new artistic journey.

Atmosphere, Motion & Making Space

Making space by playing with visual elements and harmony of colors to create an eerie environment. The reshaped and sharp letters indicate dynamism and mobility in the work and emphasize the seriousness of reform.

Summer 2022 — Hyper Visual Compositions workshop at DigitalFutures. Modelling software: Grasshopper, Rhino, and Twinmotion.
