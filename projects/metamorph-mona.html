Hyper Visual Composition

Metamorph M.O.N.A.

A composition shaped from four simple letters—M, O, N, and A—translated into spatial form through Grasshopper, Rhino, and Twinmotion.

Concept

The model emerges from four simple letters—M, O, N, A—deconstructed, reshaped, and reinterpreted in Grasshopper. Through a muted palette of cool grays infused with subtle blue and warm reflections, the piece gains an atmosphere of depth and calm tension.

The tilted and oblique forms convey momentum. They lean, flow, and stretch, capturing a sense of transformation and emotional motion. Their sharp edges speak of reform, while their fluid silhouettes suggest a search for identity.

Coming from outside the architectural discipline, my journey into design is guided by curiosity rather than tradition. This work reflects that path—restless, expressive, and constantly evolving. Metamorph M.O.N.A. becomes both a personal signature and a conceptual transformation, shaped by movement, emotion, and color.
