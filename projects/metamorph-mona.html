Metamorph M.O.N.A.

A generative composition built from the letters of my name, exploring material intelligence, motion, and atmosphere through Grasshopper-based forms and controlled color palettes.

Concept & Letter Morphology

The models in this work were created using letters of the English alphabet in Grasshopper. Four letters were used to form this model. By applying a limited palette of cool grays, complemented by selective blue and warm tones, the piece gains depth and atmosphere.

The oblique forms convey a sense of dynamism and motion, emphasizing fluidity and transformation. The model consists of the letters M, O, N, and A — representing my name, Mona.

My background is not in architecture. However, my curiosity and passion for this field led me to explore it through design. This concept reflects my evolving interest, expressing movement, creativity, and direction in this artistic journey.

Atmosphere, Motion & Making Space

Making space by playing with visual elements and color harmony to create an eerie environment. The reshaped sharp letters indicate dynamism, mobility, and reform.

Summer 2022 — Hyper Visual Compositions workshop at DigitalFutures.
Software: Grasshopper, Rhino, Twinmotion.
