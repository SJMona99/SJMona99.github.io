Hyper Visual Composition

Metamorph M.O.N.A.

Letterforms transformed into spatial prototypes — a reflective landscape where typography, motion, and atmosphere merge.

Concept

The models in this work were created using letters of the English alphabet in Grasshopper. Four letters — M, O, N, and A — form the base geometry, representing my name, Mona.

By applying a limited palette of cool grays, complemented by selective blue and warm tones, the piece gains both depth and atmosphere. This restrained use of color creates a professional, cohesive, and immersive environment.

The oblique forms convey a sense of dynamism and motion, emphasizing fluidity and transformation throughout the composition. The reshaped, sharp letters indicate mobility and highlight a seriousness of reform.

My background is not in architecture; however, curiosity and passion for this field led me to explore it through design. Metamorph M.O.N.A. reflects my evolving interest, expressing the strong sense of movement, creativity, and direction that guide me in this new artistic journey.

Summer 2022 — Hyper Visual Compositions workshop at DigitalFUTURES
Modelling software: Grasshopper, Rhino, Twinmotion

Process & Variations

Making space by playing with visual elements and the harmony of colors to create an eerie, reflective environment. These frames capture different stages of the letterforms as they shift between object, landscape, and light.
